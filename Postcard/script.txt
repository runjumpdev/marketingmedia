Run Jump Dev is a people powered nonprofit created to grow and cultivate the local game development community in Lexington, KY. Seeded with a raw passion for creating games, we host meetups, talks and jams to bolster our skills and connections.

Run Jump Dev strives to raise local awareness of game industry concerns. We are commited to educating, promoting, retaining local talent, bringing new talent to the area and strengthening the bonds between game developers.

Strong developers build strong games and successful companies in the Lexington, KY metro area.
