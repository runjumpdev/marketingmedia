RunJumpDev is a growing non-profit organization that was started to discover talent and create a community of the game developers in Lexington, KY. With a raw passion for creating interactive media, we host weekly meetups, monthly talks and jams to learn new skills and network with other developers.

RunJumpDev's focus is to raise local awareness about those interested in the game industry. We are committed to educating, promoting, retaining local talent, finding new talent and strengthening the bonds between game developers.

Strong developers build strong games and start successful companies in the city of Lexington.
